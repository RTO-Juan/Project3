Guide on the Basics of Playing Aatrox

Welcome to my guide on how to play Aatrox in the Top Lane.

I have been playing League of Legends since 2020 and have played top this entire time.

I'm not too proud of my rank but the highest I have reached is Emerald One -87 LP.

A quick intro to League of Legends: it's an online MOBA game launched in 2009, primarily played on PC. The game has over 50 million active monthly players worldwide.

The game takes place in an arena called Summoner’s Rift. It has 5 distinct roles: Top, Jungle, Mid, Bot, and Support — each with its own responsibilities. We'll focus on the Top Lane.

More specifically, we'll be focusing on the Champion Aatrox. Aatrox is classified in the Fighter class.

The first thing you should know when playing Aatrox is which runes to take.

Aatrox almost always uses the Precision tree as his primary and Resolve tree as his secondary.

Precision

  • Conqueror
  • Triumph
  • Alacrity
  • Last Stand

Resolve

  • Overgrowth
  • Second Wind

At the start of every League of Legends game, each player is given 500 gold to buy items. As a Top laner, your starting item is highly matchup-dependent.

You can either buy Doran's Blade or Doran's Shield. Generally, Aatrox should always take Doran’s Blade because he is a fighter.

With that, you now have a quick and simple guide on how to play Aatrox in the Top Lane.

Good luck, and I’ll see you on the Rift!
